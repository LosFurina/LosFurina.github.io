# 使役动词（Causative Verbs）

## 1. make + sb + do

表示“强制、迫使”。

- The manager made me redo the report.

## 2. let + sb + do

表示“允许”。

- My parents let me choose my own major.

## 3. have + sb + do / have sth done

表示“安排某人做”或“让某事被完成”。

- I had the team check the deployment.
- I had my laptop repaired.

## 4. get + sb + to do / get sth done

语气比 make 更柔和，强调“促成”。

- I got him to review the script.
- I got the issue fixed.
