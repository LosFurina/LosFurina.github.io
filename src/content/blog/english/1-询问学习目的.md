# 1. 常见问题

- What's your purpose of learning this field?
- What do you plan to do in the next two years?
- Why did you choose this direction?

# 2. 回答模板（学习目标）

In the next two years, I plan to strengthen my engineering foundation in cloud infrastructure, automation, and Linux operations. I want to build practical skills that can be applied in real production environments.

# 3. 回答模板（方向选择）

I chose this direction because it matches both my background and long-term career goal. I enjoy solving real system problems and improving delivery efficiency through automation.

# 4. 回答模板（职业计划）

After this learning stage, I plan to pursue a DevOps/Infrastructure intern role and focus on CI/CD, observability, and deployment reliability.

# 5. 可替换关键词

- engineering foundation
- production environment
- delivery efficiency
- reliability
- DevOps intern
